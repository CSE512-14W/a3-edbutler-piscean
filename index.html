    Music consists of melody (typically, the words being sung) and chords (the guitar and/or keyboards playing in the background). As a song plays, the sequence of chords played is called its “chord progression”.

    This visualization shows the chord progressions for several popular songs on a chord diagram. For instance, If a song has a chord progression (in its verse, chorus, etc) that goes from the tonic (1 chord) to the dominant (5 chord), then there is an arc from the “1” group to the “2” group in the above diagram. The total number of such transitions is shown in the above diagram.
